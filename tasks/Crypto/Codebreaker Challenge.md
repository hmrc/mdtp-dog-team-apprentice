Codebreaker
====================

**Technology:** Any

**Difficulty**: Medium

**Duration**: 4-8 hours

**Relevant concepts**: freq analysis

**Prerequisites** Decryption, Encryption

Goal
----
Reveal their secrets, protect your own. You need to come up with a text and a cipher indivdiually. 

You'll need to swap cipher texts with each other. The one whose cipher takes the longest to break wins.
